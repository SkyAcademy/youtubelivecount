Privacy Policy

Livecounts.net is committed to protecting your privacy. We will never sell your information to any third party. Details of how Livecounts collects data are outlined below.

Analytics

Livecounts uses StatCounter and Google Analytics to track this site's statistics. We use StatCounter to track total page loads and Google Analytics to count channel impressions and unique users.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files generated by websites typically to store user settings on your computer. StatCounter uses cookies to determine whether visitors are first-time or returning. Similarly, Google Analytics uses cookies to identify unique users and sessions. The Twitter buttons at the bottom of the Livecounts main page use cookies to identify browser information. More information can be found at the aforementioned services' respective privacy policies.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • StatCounter Privacy Policy
  • Twitter Privacy Policy

Personal information

StatCounter, Google Analytics, and Twitter use IP addresses to identify users, record page views, and more. The Livecounts team will never use your IP address to personally identify you or track your browsing activity.
